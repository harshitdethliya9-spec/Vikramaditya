What We Offer

World-Class Venue Facilities

Every detail at Vikram Aditya Pallace is designed to make your wedding, engagement, or corporate event completely seamless and luxurious.

Primary Venue

Lush Green Marriage Garden

A massive, beautifully landscaped garden perfect for grand weddings, receptions, theme events, and outdoor seating setups. Dynamic lighting layouts supported.

Indoor Event

Grand Banquet Hall

A spacious air-conditioned indoor banquet hall for hosting wedding stages, sagai (engagements), birthday celebrations, and dining tables. Separate toilet washrooms attached.

4 Rooms

Premium AC Accommodation

4 luxurious, spacious AC bedrooms with attached clean modern bathrooms. Perfect for groom, bride, and immediate family members to prepare and relax.

5 Rooms

Standard Non-AC Rooms

5 comfortable, well-ventilated Non-AC guest rooms with attached washrooms. Offers a cosy and clean stay environment for wedding guests and relatives.

60+ Cars

Spacious Safe Parking

Dedicated, secure, and hassle-free inside parking capacity for 60+ cars, ensuring your esteemed guests do not have to worry about vehicle security.

24/7 Booking

Flexible booking & Timings

While our physical office hours are from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, booking services and onsite function support remain open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
